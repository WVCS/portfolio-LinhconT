Castle Interactive

Castle Interactive is a company I plan on running. It specializes in manufactiring video games. Games that I mainly plan on are primarily first person shooters that travel across all time zones, such as Modern, the two World Wars, the Future, Vietnam, and more. One of the games that I am currently working on is a game called "Frontlines", where you control a military operator in an international counter-terrorist unit called the ICTC, or the International Counter-Terrorist Coalition, fighting a terrorist cell called the Syndicate of the Right World. You travel around the world, capturing outposts that they own. You can also control another operator of another unit called the ITF, or the Intellect Task Force. They are fighting a Marxist-Leninist uprising in Vietnam, which in the game is a democratic-republic state. The game will run on the Unreal 4 Engine, and is planning on being released for PC and Console, but hopes are being set high for PC due to any possible complications on console.
